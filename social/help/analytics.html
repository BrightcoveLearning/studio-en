Social Analytics

Monitor the social performance of your videos.

Dashboard

Displays an overview of video performance from your social media accounts.

  1. Select a time span.
  2. Click Apply to update the report.
  3. Video Views at a Glance - Video views from your social media accounts for a period of time
    • My Sites & Apps - Video view counted when a video begins to play; opens the Video Cloud Analytics Performance Report
    • Facebook - Video view counted when a video is watched for at least 3 seconds
    • YouTube - Video view counted when a video is watched for "around" 30 seconds
  4. Top destinations - Top 5 destinations with the most views for a period of time
  5. Top Videos - Top 5 videos with the most views for a period of time; click the video title to open the social views analytics
  6. Social Views - Video views for the videos posted to your social media accounts for a period of time; hover over a line in the graph to display data for that date

Learn more

Social Views

Displays video views for videos posted to your social media accounts for a period of time.

  1. Select a time span.
  2. Click Apply to update the report.
  3. Social Views - Video views for videos posted to your social media accounts for a period of time; typically, there is a 24-48+ hour delay before social platforms update the views and engagement data in their APIs
    • My Sites & Apps - Video views from Video Cloud players and apps
    • Facebook - Video views on Facebook
    • YouTube - Video views on YouTube
    • Total - Total number of video views across all platforms
  4. Social views details - Video view detail by Views , Average Time Watched, Average % Watched.
  5. Exporting data - Download report data as either a CSV or Excel file

Learn more

Engagement

Displays social engagement (Likes, Shares, Retweets, etc.) for videos shared to social media accounts.

  1. Select a time span.
  2. Click Apply to update the report.
  3. Social Engagement - User engagement for videos posted to your social media accounts for a period of time period; typically, there is a 24-48+ hour delay before social platforms update the views and engagement data in their APIs
    • Twitter - Number of Retweets and Favorites
    • Facebook - Number of Likes, Comments and Shares
    • YouTube - Number of Comments, Likes, Dislikes and Shares
  4. Social Engagement by Video - Detailed view data for each video; Use the column selector to select the columns to display
    For each video, the following will be displayed:
    • Video name and duration
    • Video ID
    • Created date
    • Selected columns
  5. Exporting data - Download report data as either a CSV or Excel file
