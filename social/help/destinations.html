Destinations

Create destinations to publish videos to YouTube, Facebook and Twitter.

Create a YouTube destination

To create a YouTube destination, follow these steps:

  1. Click Add Destination.
  2. Click Sign in with YouTube.
  3. Login to your Google account associated with your YouTube channel.
  4. A dialog will open requesting permission to manage your YouTube account.
  5. Click Allow.
  6. Give the destination a unique, descriptive name. Click Add.
  7. See the Setting YouTube defaults section for information on configuring default values.
  8. Click Destinations to refresh the page. The value below the destination name is the destination ID.

Create a Facebook destination

To create a Facebook destination, follow these steps:

  1. Click Add Destination.
  2. Click Sign in with Facebook.
  3. Enter your Facebook credentials and Log In to your account.
  4. Click Continue to confirm that Brightcove Social will receive your profile info.
  5. Click OK to confirm that Brightcove Social will be able to manage and publish your pages and access your Page and App insights.
  6. Give the destination a unique, descriptive name. Click Add.
  7. See the Setting Facebook defaults section for information on configuring default values.
  8. Click Destinations to refresh the page. The value below the destination name is the destination id.

Learn more

Create a Twitter destination

To create a Twitter destination, follow these steps:

  1. Click Add Destination.
  2. Select Sign in with Twitter.
  3. Login to the Twitter account you want to post videos to. Click Authorize App.
  4. Give the destination a unique, descriptive name. Click Add.
  5. See the Setting Twitter defaults section for information on configuring default values.
  6. Click Destinations to refresh the page. The value below the destination name is the destination id.

Learn more

Delete a destination

To delete a destination, follow these steps:

  1. In the left navigation, click Destinations to see a list of your current destinations.
  2. Click the destination name to delete.
  3. In the destination detail, click and then click Delete to confirm the deletion.
    4. Note: The delete icon will not appear if there are Auto Sync jobs created that use the destination. You must remove the Auto Sync jobs before deleting the destination.

Reconnect to a destination
You may see an error message if the Brightcove permissions are removed from the social media account or when the destination credentials are no longer valid.

To resolve the issue:

  1. Click the destination name.
  2. Click the Reconnect link at the top of the page.
  3. You may be prompted to re-authenticate with the social media account.
