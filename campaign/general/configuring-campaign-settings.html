To configure the Campaign settings, open the Campaign module and click Settings in the left navigation. The following settings can be configured:

  • Email Notifications

Configuring email notifications

The Email Notifications section can be configured to send email notifications when there is an issue with Campaign. Multiple addresses can be added and should be separated with a comma.
