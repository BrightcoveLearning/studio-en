The table below outlines the cookies used by Video Cloud Studio. Several factors affect the number of cookies that are deployed in connection with your use of Video Cloud Studio:

  • If you access Video Cloud Studio via the Customer Login option on the Brightcove home page, you will see additional cookies
  • The type of Video Cloud account that you have (Enterprise, Professional or Express)
Note: The privacy policy governing the Brightcove homepage is found here.

Your browser must allow cookies in order to access Video Cloud Studio.
