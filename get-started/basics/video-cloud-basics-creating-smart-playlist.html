Prerequisites

  • You have uploaded videos to your Video Cloud account
  • You have added some tags to your videos

Steps

  1. In the primary navigation, open the Players module.
  2. In the side navigation panel, click the Create playlist link.
  3. Set the Playlist name to Birds or another appropriate name.
  4. In the Playlist type section, click on Smart playlist.
  5. Click Create playlist.
  6. Select a Parameter of Tags.
  7. Set the operator to Match any.
  8. Enter birds for the tag (or whatever the appropriate tag may be) and press enter.
  9. Click Apply.
  10. Click the Overview tab.
  11. Check Show Playlist in Sidebar to have the playlist appear in the sidebar.
  12. Close the panel by clicking the X.
  13. Click the playlist name just created in the side navigation panel and confirm the correct videos have been added to the playlist.
Note: For complete details on creating a smart playlist, see Creating and Managing Playlists.
