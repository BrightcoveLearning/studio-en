Prerequisites

  • You have uploaded videos to your Video Cloud account

Steps

  1. In the primary navigation, open the Media module.
  2. Click on a video title to open the video properties page.
  3. The video properties are organized into sections. To edit video properties, click Edit or double-click in a section.
  4. Click Save to save the changes.
