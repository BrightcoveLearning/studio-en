Steps

  1. In Video Cloud Studio, open the Analytics module. The Performance report will display.
  2. To specify the time period for the report, use the date selector at the top of the page.
  3. Select to view the Performance by Video dimension.
  4. Click on the Video Views column to sort the data.

The search field at the top of the page can be used to search for a specific video.
