Steps

  1. In Video Cloud Studio, click Upload to open the Upload module.
  2. Click Browse for Videos and browse and select one or more files to upload or Drag and Drop files from the file system to the Upload module.
  3. The files will begin processing. Remain on the Upload module page (and don't refresh the page) until all videos have a status of Processing Complete.
  4. Click View and edit video details to view the video and edit the video properties in the Media module.
