How it works

Chapters make it easier for viewers to go to a specific point in a video. This is especially helpful for longer form videos. Rather than scrubbing through the video, the chapter menu can be used to jump to a specific point in the video.

Using chapters

When a Brightcove user uses chapters, that increases engagement by empowering viewers to go to the part of the video they are most interested in, instead of leaving. Every click on a chapter creates rich intent data about what that viewer wants to see.

Adding a new chapter

Note: In order to create chapters, you first need to connect your Brightcove account with HapYak. (Link)

To add a new chapter, follow these steps:

  1. In the bottom toolbar, click on Chapter Menu.
  2. Chapter Menu allows Brightcove users to see lists of all the Bookmarks. You can change the name of Chapters to the name that you wish or delete the text entirely to show a Chapter Menu Icon.
