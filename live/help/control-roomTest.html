Control Room

In this page, you will be able to:

Begin streaming

To connect the video stream to your encoder:

  1. Click in the Streaming Endpoint (RTMP URL) and paste it into your encoder.
  2. Click in the Stream Name and paste it into your encoder.
View information about the live event

The Manage Events page displays three main tabs:

  • Live: Lists live events that are in progress.
  • Standby: Lists recurring events that are in standby mode.
  • Completed: Lists completed events.
