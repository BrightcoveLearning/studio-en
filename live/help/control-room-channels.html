The Control Room page is used to view/edit information about the live channel.

Begin Streaming

To connect the video stream to your encoder:

  1. Click the Activate button. Channels must be activated before the streaming can begin.
  2. Open your encoder to start streaming.
  3. Click Copy in the Streaming Endpoint (RTMP URL) and paste it into your encoder.
  4. Click Copy in the Stream Name and paste it into your encoder.
  5. With that information, start the streaming on your encoder, and the video will appear in the Control Room in a few seconds.
  6. To stop the channel, click on Cancel Stream.

Live channel information

The Control Room page displays five main sections:

  • Channel Information - Displays information about the event as Event Status, Current Viewers, and Elapsed Time.
  • Channel Details - Displays reference detasils of the channel as Channel Name, Video ID, and Short Description.
  • Channel Configuration Settings - Displays information about the configuration settings of the event as Job ID, Video ID, Status and Endpoint.
  • Monitor - Retrieves data and graphs from the current event.
  • Renditions - Displays details about the renditions that were created. Clicking on a rendition link will show the encoding settings for the rendition.
  • Clips - Displays all of the clips that were created from the live stream.
