Introduction

MAP and CRM systems are great at storing data about user behavior. They are generally less effective at analyzing that data and identifying patterns that you can act on. This is where Brightcove's Audience Insights technology can provide valuable insights into your data to help you make good business decisions.

Here are some examples of the kind of campaigns you might run based on the insights Brightcove provides:

  • Lead Qualification: Set different Segment tags to build a Lead Score in your Marketing Platform. Qualify them and move them along the funnel.
  • Ideal Customer Persona (ICP): Identify your ICP and create a segment that will report every viewer with similar behavior to run a targeted campaign. Identify viewer preferences, patterns, and engagement to cater to the specific cohort through targeted messaging and campaigns.
  • Content promotion: Engage a prospect that has watched a specific video with related content.
  • Subscription Management: Identify different segments of customers to lead them through their subscription or upgrade journey. Examples of Subscription management campaigns based on Segments are: Engage trialist, Updated Onboarding Flow, Idle users, Annual Plan Upgrades, Scheduled Cancellation Win-backs, Lost Customer Win-backs.

Set up an integration

Setting up an integration is straightforward. However, you will need some information from the third-party vendor - exactly what you need will vary for different services, but will be clearly indicated by input fields in the setup dialog. Typical requirements are "Account id", "API key", and/or "Secret key". If you are unsure how to find or obtain a required piece of information, consult the vendor's documentation or support service.

Steps

  1. Login to Studio
  2. Click the Integrations icon in the left-side navigation to open the Integrations module:
  3. Click Customer Data Platforms to go to the data platform integrations:
  4. As you see in the screenshot above, any existing integrations will be shown as a list and can be modified (see the next section). Here we will click Add Integration to create a new integration.
  5. In the Add Integration dialog, first select the service you want to integrate:
  6. Next, fill in the required information according to the service you are integrating:
  7. Click Save to complete the setup.

Modify an integration

As shown above, in the list of existing integrations, there are three icons that allow you to:

  • Edit the integration
  • Test the integration
  • Delete the integration

In some cases the Test icon will be grayed out, indicating that this functionality is not available for that particular integration.

Viewer ID Tracking plugin

The Brightcove Viewer ID tracking plugin is a key feature for content creators and video platform owners aiming to gain in-depth insights into their audience by assigning and tracking viewers through unique IDs. This tool is vital for analyzing viewer behavior, content preferences, and engagement patterns, enabling tailored content and targeted marketing strategies for enhanced viewer experience and engagement.

Create a new Player or Edit an existing Player

If you are editing an existing Campaign player, you will also want to remove the existing Campaign plugin. However, you could also create a New Player.

  1. Open the Campaign module.
  2. Deselect the Enable Campaign checkbox.
  3. Click Save and Publish your player.

Enable the Viewer ID Tracking Plugin

You can enable the Viewer ID Tracking Plugin by taking the following steps from the player details view:

  1. Open the Viewer ID Tracking tab.
  2. Select the Enable Viewer ID Tracking checkbox.
  3. Choose the 3rd Party Service from the Service dropdown.
  4. Input any additional required fields.
  5. Click Save and Publish your player.

Setup your Interactivity Player

We first have to enable your Player for Interactivity and ensure that any videos played inside of this player have “Interactivity Projects” that can receive Lead Forms. To do this follow the next steps:

  1. Open the Interactivity tab in the Player Details section on the Players Module.
  2. Select the Enable Interactivity checkbox.
  3. To enable the auto-creation of Interactivity projects, select the Options (JSON) checkbox. This reveals a textarea where you can insert a JSON configuration.
  4. Inside the textarea, insert the following JSON. 
    {
   "create": true
 }
  5. Click Save and Publish your player.

Once the changes have taken effect, any video played within this Player has a Project created automatically.

Note: When Projects are created from videos, any tags defined in the Media Module are also copied over to the new Project. It’s worth taking an extra step here to come up with a unique tag for any videos you’d like to apply a lead form to and apply them to your videos inside of the Media Module.

Setup your Lead Form

Now that you have an Interactivity Player, we need to create a Lead Form to apply to the videos you load in the Interactivity Player. To create a Lead Form follow the next steps:

  1. Open the Interactivity Module.
  2. Click to Templates > All Templates.
  3. Click New template and provide a descriptive name.
  4. On the Template details screen, you are presented with a 1-minute dummy video that you can work on.
    • If you just plan on having the lead form opening at the start of every video, or within 1 minute of playback, then you can continue with the existing dummy video.
    • If you would like a lead form to appear at a specific time above 60 seconds (such as 5 minutes into a video), then you will need to use the Replace Video button to select a video of the correct length to create your template.
  5. Use the player control bar to create a new Lead Form Annotation.
    • On the Form Source tab, copy & paste the lead form code you previously used in Campaign - or directly from your MAP.
      • Select whether or not you wish to Require Submission.
        • Selecting this option will require viewers to fill out the form before playback resumes.
        • Not selecting this option will display a `Close` button in the upper right corner of the lead form, where viewers can resume playback without filling out the form
      • Select whether or not you wish to Show form everytime.
        • If unselected, the lead form will only display if it’s determined that the viewer is not yet known.
    • Use the Time Tab to modify the time at which the Lead Form should appear.
      • This can be an exact playback time in hh:mm:ss or a percentage of playback.
      • Video playback will pause and seek the user back to this time until the user has successfully closed or submitted the lead form annotation at which point the main content will resume
    • Click Save

Apply the Lead Form across multiple projects

To apply the Lead Form across multiple projects / videos follow these steps:

  1. Edit your Lead Form template and click to the Settings panel.
  2. On Apply Templates to Projects by Tag. Define a unique tag that dictates which projects receive your Lead Form Template.
  3. Use the Apply Templates to Projects by Tag field to define a unique tag that dictates which projects receive your Lead Form Template.

    Note: If you have chosen a unique tag to apply to your videos in the Media Module. Then, use the same tag in this field to ensure your Lead Form Template is applied to the Project created by your Interactivity Player.

    Scenario: I already have Projects in my account and they don’t have my Lead Form applied or Tags associated with them.

    • If you already have projects based on your videos without tags, add the tag to these projects. The Lead Form should now appear in your Interactivity Player. Make sure the corresponding video has the tag in the Media Module.

    Scenario: I’ve applied a tag to my template, but I don’t have a Project based off of my video.

    • If you don’t have a project based on your video, add the tag you created to the video inside the Media Module and playback the video in your Interactivity Player. An Interactivity Project that contains its tags is automatically generated from your video, and your Lead Form template is automatically applied upon playback.

Setup MAPs Tracking (Marketo & HubSpot Client-Side Only)

To Setup MAPs Tracking follow these steps:

  1. On Players Module edit your Interactivity Player.
  2. In your Player Settings, click to the MAPs tab on the left menu navigation.
  3. On the MAPs page, select the Enable MAPs checkbox.
  4. Select your Service (Marketo or Hubspot) and enter Your Account ID.
  5. Click Save and Publish your player.

Publishing your Player

To access your Publishing Code from the Interactivity Module, follow these steps.

  1. On Interactivity Module, select the Project you want to publish.
  2. On the Project Details page you can preview how the Lead Form would look in player.
  3. Click Embed from the top-right to open the embed code modal.
  4. Select the Interactivity Player you’d like to embed your project.
  5. Copy the JavaScript code snippet from this window and paste it into your page.
