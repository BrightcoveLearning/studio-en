Overview

Marketing Comms Insights offers a powerful tool to track and enhance the effectiveness of your video content. It provides valuable insights into audience behavior, campaign performance, and content engagement. With these insights, you can make informed, data-driven decisions to optimize your video strategies and maximize the impact of your marketing efforts.

Set up

Publisher insights is available to Media Studio Premium customers. Contact your Customer Success Manager to enable your account.

Navigation

  1. Log in to your Video Cloud account.
  2. Click on Analytics
  3. On the left navigation menu, Click Insights

Engagement

The Engagement dashboard provides critical metrics to understand how viewers interact with video content over time. The visual data trends help marketing and content teams identify patterns, evaluate campaign effectiveness, and adjust strategies to increase engagement and retention. The dashboard also offers filtering options, like viewing engagement by marketing campaigns, which allows teams to measure the success of individual efforts.

You can find more information about this tab here.

Content

The Content dashboard provides a library overview that enables teams to understand which videos resonate most with viewers and adjust their content offerings to match audience preferences.
