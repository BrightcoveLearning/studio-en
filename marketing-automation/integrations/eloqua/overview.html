Overview

This integration enables you to capture valuable insights into how your audience engages with video content.

With two powerful data integration options, Viewing Data Integration and Contact Object Integration, you can choose between detailed, event-level tracking or high-level audience insights to enhance your Eloqua workflows.

Viewing Data vs Contact Object Integration

The table below outlines the key differences between Viewing Data Integration and Contact Object Integration.

Feature Viewing Data Integration Contact Object Integration
Granularity Granular viewing data captured per individual event and tracked by day. Includes detailed engagement metrics. Aggregated data at the contact level, summarizing viewing activity over the past 3 months or 1 year.
Use Cases Primarily used by Marketing & Enterprise Customers who need detailed insights into whether a specific video was watched on a specific date for lead scoring, segmentation, and personalized marketing. Primarily used by Media Customers who need a broad understanding of viewer consumption patterns to build targeted campaigns for a group of customers.

Viewing Data integration

The Viewing Data Integration feature in Brightcove enables granular, event-based tracking of viewer interactions with video content. This integration is designed to help marketing teams and enterprise customers analyze engagement patterns, optimize lead scoring, and personalize campaigns based on actual video consumption behavior.

Whether you're looking to improve marketing automation workflows, optimize content performance, or enhance audience segmentation, Viewing Data Integration provides actionable insights that help turn video engagement into measurable business outcomes.
