Overview

This document details the properties used by the Client-Side connection for the viewing data integration.

Client-Side Properties


Field Description
provider=brightcove Brightcove will be displayed as the provider.
videoId={VIDEO_ID} Brightcove video asset ID.
videoName={URL_ENCODED_VIDEO_NAME} Title of the video asset.
account={BC_ACCOUNT_ID} Brightcove account ID.
player={BC_PLAYER_ID} Brightcove player ID.
timeWatched={SECONDS_WATCHED} Number of seconds watched by the viewer.
percentile={25|50|75|95} For each segment (25%/50%/75%/95%) an event is sent as a custom object.
Page URL is obtained by the MAP The page URL is not passed through the Brightcove plugin; it is obtained directly by the map from the site.
